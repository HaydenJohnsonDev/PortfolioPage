About Me

Finally deciding to go the route of Software Engineering was a decision long in the making.

My brother and I graduated to the game development platform Unity, and quickly fell in love with problem solving in order to make engaging games using the C# language. during that time we made:

  • Assimilation Survival Game (In Progress)
  • And Many Other Small Projects

At that point, we were doing all of that just for our own enjoyment, I hadn't really thought of pursueing it full time. My family convinced me to look into the options and I later found Thinkful. I am now studying to be a Software Engineer, and I cannot wait to get into the field.
